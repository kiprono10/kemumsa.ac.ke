About KeMUMSA

Our Mission, Vision, and Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2010, the Kenya Methodist University Medical Students Association (KeMUMSA) has grown from a small group of passionate medical students to a vibrant community of over 500 members. Our journey began with a simple goal: to create a supportive network for medical students navigating the challenges of medical education.

Today, we stand as one of the most active student associations at Kenya Methodist University, recognized for our commitment to academic excellence, professional development, and community service.

14 Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To foster academic excellence, professional growth, and ethical practice among medical students through comprehensive support, mentorship, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading medical students association in Kenya, producing competent, compassionate, and community-oriented healthcare professionals.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in academic and professional development

Compassion

Caring for patients and communities with empathy and understanding

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals and support one another

Integrity

Upholding ethical standards and professional conduct

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and approaches in medical education

Service

Dedicated to serving our communities and improving public health
